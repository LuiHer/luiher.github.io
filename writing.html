Medical and Scientific Writing


I have communicated scientific concepts to a wide variety of audiences throughout my career. I look forward to continuing in scientific and medical communications. I have 10 years of experience in the biomedical sciences as a researcher. I also have 2 years in the pharmaceutical industry. I am trained in Good Manufacturing Practices for radiopharmaceuticals. This includes knowledge of FDA, EMA, and ICH guidelines. I am certified in Good Clinical Practice (GCP) by the US National Institutes of Drug Abuse (NIDA). I have co-written two research grants. I also have experience writing technical documentation such as presentations, journal articles, protocols, and grant applications. As you consider me for your scientific and medical writing needs, feel free to explore my CV. I look forward to discussing my skills and experience in more detail in an interview.

Science Communication and Outreach


I have co-organized science education events. During my Ph.D., I taught students research skills as a co-director of a summer research program. Some of our workshops included how to dissect a journal article and how to create a research poster. I taught biology to entry-level medical students at the Santo Domingo Institute of Technology (INTEC) in the Dominican Republic. You can also find my science communication project on Instagram @synaptic.island.
